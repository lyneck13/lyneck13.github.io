About

This website was created to provide one place that a person could find the top free multimedia software. GIMP 2.8 and Inkscape are image and graphic editors. Synfig and Blender are 2D and 3D animation software. Mixxx help with audio files and Avidemux a video editor. There are links to the software downloads, the user manuals, as well as, beginner, intermediate and advanced tutorials. I hope this is as helpful to you and as it was for me.
